Yu (Lucas) Wu

I am a M.S. in Robotics student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Shubham Tulsiani. My research interests are in 3D computer vision and generative models. I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at CMU as well. Previously, I worked with Prof. Maxim Likhachev and Prof. Jiaoyang Li on multi-agent path finding (e.g. MAPF).
