Yu (Lucas) Wu

I am a senior undergrad at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in CS. I am currently working on 3D computer vision supervised by Prof. Shubham Tulsiani. Before that, I worked with Prof. Maxim Likhachev and Prof. Jiaoyang Li on multi-agent motion planning (e.g. MAPF).

In general, I am interested in reconstructing and generating the 3D visual world we live in. I am also interested in exploring robotics and how it could interact with 3D vision.
